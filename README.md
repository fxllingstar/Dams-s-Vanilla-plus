# Dams's Vanilla+ **DVPlus**

DVPlus is a Paper plugin designed to enhance the vanilla Minecraft experience with immersive mechanics, survival utility, and atmospheric features. It focuses on adding depth to existing blocks and items without breaking the core vanilla feel.



---

## Key features


### "✦ Equipment" Luminous Equipment

Make your gear work as a flashlight using the Smithing Table

* **The Recipe:** Combine a **Glow Item Frame** (Template), any **Tool/Armor** (Base), and a **Glow Ink Sac** (Addition).

* **Dynamic Lighting:** Luminous items emit a real-time light level of 13 around the player. This uses **client-side block updates** (`sendBlockChange`), ensuring no physical blocks are modified in the world.

* **Notifications:** Players receive a status update every 5 minutes as the luminosity fades.

* **Multi-Slot Support:** Works in the main hand, off-hand, and all armor slots.

**NOTE: Works on swords, pickaxes, shovels, hoes, axes and  all the armor.** 

### Messenger Parrots
Utilize your feathered friends to deliver messages across the world.
* **Command:** `/parrot deliver <message>`
* A unique, atmospheric way to communicate with other players in survival.


###  Campfire Purification
A survival-friendly way to obtain leather in the early game.
* Right-click a **Campfire** or **Soul Campfire** while holding **Rotten Flesh**.
* The flesh is consumed and, after 2 minutes of smoking,a piece of **Leather** is dropped at the campfire.

###  Stonecutter Sharpening
Give your tools a temporary edge at the cost of their lifespan.
* Right-click a **Stonecutter** while holding a Pickaxe or Sword.
* **Cost:** 10 Durability points.
* **Reward:** Pickaxes receive **Haste I** and Swords receive **Strength I** for 10 minutes.

### Frost-Bound Cauldrons
Automated ice farming for cold-climate.
* Cauldrons left in cold biomes have a chance to freeze overnight, turning into **Blue Ice**.
